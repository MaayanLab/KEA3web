Submit

Submit a query with one gene or protein symbol per row in the text box, or upload a text file with one gene or protein symbol per line. KEA3 accepts HGNC-approved gene symbols (but is not case-sensitive) and will discard probe names, transcript IDs, and other unrecognizable IDs that have not been converted to gene symbols. You can use the example gene set available as a link above the submission box to gain familiarity with the expected format. After submitting your query, you will be presented with the results page. Most queries are processed in less than 30 seconds.
